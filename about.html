About Me

This is me.

Hi everyone!

My name is Javier Garrido López and I am an Electronic Engineer. In this webpage, I'll be sharing to you some of the projects I've done. Also, you can contact me and talk to me if you wish.



I live in a small village called Archena, in Murcia, Spain. I was born here in 1999. While I was studying at school, I was always interested in how things worked and how they were made. As a result, I started to like everything related to Technology and Engenieering, generally speaking. Specially, I loved the area related to electronics and computers.

Therefore, after finishing my school studies in a bilingual high school, from 2017 to 2021 I studied Electronics and Automatic Industrial Engineering degree in the University of Cartagena (UPCT). During that time, I learned a lot of things about what really passioniates me: electronics and programming. I made many projects for the University and got the Extraordinary End of Career Award for the highest record in my promotion. I'm currently studying Industrial Engineering Master's degree, and I hope becoming University professor someday.

Besides, I also love music and playing the electric and Spanish guitar.
